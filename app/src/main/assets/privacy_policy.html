Notifications: The notifications you choose to store are safely stored in Firebase.

We use your data to enhance your experience with Notify Vault in the following ways:

We care about your privacy. Your personal data will never be shared with third parties. We only use your information to make your experience with the app better.

Data Security

Your data security is very important to us. We store notifications safely using Firebase, and we take steps to protect your information. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure. While we cannot guarantee absolute security, we are committed to continuously improving our protection measures to keep your information safe.

User Rights

We respect your rights over your data. You have the following options to manage your information:

  • You can delete all stored notifications at any time through the app settings.
  • You may restore deleted notifications unless they have been permanently removed.
  • You have full control over your auto-delete preferences and can customize them as needed.

Uninstallation and Reinstallation

Please note that if you uninstall and later reinstall Notify Vault, all stored notifications will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered. We suggest reviewing your notifications before uninstalling to ensure no important data is lost.
