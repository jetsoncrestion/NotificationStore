Privacy Policy

Introduction

At Notify: Notifications History, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information. We are dedicated to keeping your data safe and ensuring that your experience with the app remains secure and transparent.

Data Collection

To provide you with the best experience, Notify: Notifications History Saver collects and stores the following information:

Notifications: The notifications you choose to store are safely stored in Firebase.

Preferences: Your app preferences, such as auto-delete settings and selected apps, are stored locally on your device to help personalize your experience.

Data Usage

We use your data to enhance your experience with Notify: Notifications History Saver in the following ways:

  • Displaying and managing your notifications within the app to help you stay organized.
  • Improving your overall experience by offering useful features like search and filtering.

Data Sharing

We care about your privacy. Your personal data will never be shared with third parties. We only use your information to make your experience with the app better.

Data Security

Your data security is very important to us. We store notifications safely using Firebase, and we take steps to protect your information. However, please note that no method of data transmission or storage is completely secure. While we cannot guarantee absolute security, we are committed to continuously improving our protection measures to keep your information safe.

User Rights

We respect your rights over your data. You have the following options to manage your information:

  • You can delete all stored notifications at any time through the app settings.
  • You may restore deleted notifications unless they have been permanently removed.
  • You have full control over your auto-delete preferences and can customize them as needed.
  • Deleted notifications will be stored in a recycle bin for easier recovery. You can choose auto-delete settings for these notifications, such as daily, weekly, or monthly deletion.

Data Retention

Notifications and associated data are securely stored in Firebase for as long as you continue using Notify: Notifications History Saver. Additionally, notifications stored on the main screen will be retained for up to 3 months (90 days). After this period, they will be automatically deleted to maintain storage efficiency and ensure privacy.

If you choose to delete notifications or uninstall the app, the associated data will be permanently removed. Please be assured that we do not retain any of your data after you delete it or uninstall the app. We are committed to respecting your choices and ensuring your data remains under your control.

Third-Party Services

To provide you with the best possible experience, Notify: Notifications History Saver uses trusted third-party services, such as Firebase, for essential features. These services may process or store data as part of their operations, and we have carefully chosen them to ensure they adhere to the highest standards of security and privacy.

We encourage you to learn more about Firebase's privacy practices by visiting their Privacy Policy. Rest assured, we are committed to protecting your data and working with partners who share our dedication to user privacy.

Account

Notify: Notifications History Saver does not require user registration. Instead, we use a unique device ID to securely associate your data with your device. This approach ensures that you can access all app features without the need to provide personal information or create an account.

The unique device ID allows us to store and manage your data in Firebase efficiently while maintaining your privacy. All data is securely stored, and no personal details are linked to the device ID.

Uninstallation and Reinstallation

Please note that if you uninstall and later reinstall Notify: Notifications History Saver, all stored notifications will be permanently erased and cannot be recovered. We kindly suggest reviewing your notifications before uninstalling to ensure no important data is lost.

When you wish to uninstall the app, please make sure to delete all notifications associated with Notify: Notifications History Saver to avoid any potential loss of important information.

Amendments

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to improve our service. Any changes will be clearly reflected within the app. By continuing to use Notify: Notification History Saver after such updates, you kindly acknowledge your acceptance of the revised policy. We recommend reviewing the policy from time to time to stay informed.
